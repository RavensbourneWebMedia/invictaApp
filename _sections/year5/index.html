We are MacArthur Class and we have set up a mini-enterprise specialising in South American food. Our business, Exquisite Foreign Foods Ltd., has created a unique product; a beautiful book that combines travel writing with fantastic recipes that will challenge your cooking skills and introduce you to new tastes.
